About Me

I am a 2nd year Ph.D. student in Industrial Engineering (concentrated in Human Factors) , and an M.S. in Cognitive Psychology at Purdue University - West Lafayette, IN. I am also the lab manager of the NHanCE (Next-generation Human-systems and Cognitive Engineering) Research Lab.

Research Interests

  • Autonomous driving
  • Human-automation interaction
  • Aging and technology
  • Multisensory/multimodal information processing
  • Adaptive automation
